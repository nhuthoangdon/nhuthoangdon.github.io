A smart Health App that makes it easier when you have a symptom. An all-in-one process of checking symptoms, smartly - booking doctor appointments - managing symptoms & medication.

UI COMPONENTS

UI/UX EXPLAINED

PROFILE

Easy switching between Medical & Personal info. Completion of Profile is not compulsory; however encouraged as the datas might enhance the assessment's accuracy. Relevant info will be prefilled if users choose to fill in the Patient Form online when booking a doctor appointment.

HOME - INPUT SYMPTOMS

Options to check for someone else
Default: self checking. If check for someone else, user will be prompted to fill in some basic info of the person being checked for.

2 ways of inputing symptoms
Traditional input via text field (with search suggestions) for intuitiveness & Visualised Human body for optimised user experience when picking on the troubled body part(s).

RESULT

Result shows possibilities of the conditions, medical advice, disease info & options to book Doctor appointment / add symptom(s) to watch list for tracking.

BOOK APPOINTMENT

Doctors listing with availability
Doctors listing with key info and pickable date/time enable users to quickly schedule an appointment without going through too many steps.

Refined booking process
Booking can be done right away for users who have completed all the relevant fields in their profile. Those haven't will be asked to quickly provide necessary info (e.g. Insurance) and choose whether they want to complete the patient form online, or at the doctor's office.

Filter
If user book from the assessment result, the system will automatically filtered the Doctor listing base on Expertise - Distance - Rating - Availability accordingly. Filter criterias will be displayed as words with 2 key criterias (e.g. 'Nearest Primary Care Doctors', 'Best Dermatologists', 'Flexible schedule Eye Doctors', etc.) as it is friendlier for users to pickup how the Doctor listing works.

APPOINTMENTS LISTING

Expandable blocks
Each appointment is displayed in a separate default collapsed block with key info visible. Confirmed upcoming appointments will be on top.

Status & Doctor's note
An appointment is officially confirmed after the doctor has reviewed and confirmed it. If the doctor has any note for the patient, it will be showed half visible in the collapsed block (doctor's note is also considered as key info - half visible display for real estate saving).

Reschedule & cancel
Appointments canceling is possible, however not encouraged. Thus, the option to cancel will be visible inside the 'Reschedule' option.

SYMPTOMS TRACKING

Categorised symptoms
Symptoms are grouped within their causes: diseases that have been diagnosed by doctors, diseases from assessment results, unknown (symptoms that have not been diagnosed or checked).

Medications info
Users can add medications to each group of symptoms for better management.

Colour-visualised progression
Each symptom has its own status of progression. Status of progression is visualised with colours indicating 3 states: Red for getting worse, Neutral for progressing, Green for getting better. Overall progression of all symptoms within one group will be reflected by the group's outline colour.

Symptom tracking & detail
Symptom detail screen allows users to add progress, see medication info (if any) and tracking history. Users are able to track the symptom progress daily. Progression is visualised with a graphic chart.
