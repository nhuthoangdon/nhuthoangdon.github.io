CONCEPT

Schecker is my idea of a smart Health App that checks symptoms smartly, manages them, and connects patients to their doctors & vice versa.

HOW IT WORKS
Check Symptom(s)

Schecker's algorithm works like a real doctor in which the system will ask relevant questions considering all aspects of the possible causes to the user's condition(s), and analyses the answers to provide a result as accurate as possible.

Get Result

Result includes information about the user's symptom(s), prediction of the condition(s)/disease(s), further assessments/medical tests needed, etc. All in a manner that everyone who doesn't speak 'medical terminology' can understand.

Book Appointment

Book an appointment with Schecker's partnered Doctors/Clinics/Hospitals, with a report of the result sent beforehand.

Watch Symptom(s)

Keep track of symptom(s). The system will remind user to update progress of his/her symptom(s), if a symptom occurs for too long without improving, user will be urged to get checked by a healthcare professional asap.

VISION

Some more features I've thought of incorporating into the prototype

Results Matching

When user checks and books appointments via Schecker, a report of his/her questions & answers will be sent to the doctor, the doctor will then have a look and make a judgement of his/her own, as well as notify/update to the patient of any changes or additional advices prior to the appointments. Then, when an official diagnosis is made, it will be automatically updated in the app together with the prescribed medication and treatments.

Data will be used to improve Schecker's AI.

Progress Management

Tracks progress of symptoms (diagnosed & undiagnosed) and treatments.

Medication management

This links with the Progress Management feature, it gives info of drugs and their interactions with each other and the body, as well as reminding and making sure medication is taken on time.

Pets Version

Schecker's version for pets.

Please check out my interactive prototype with the basic flow. For this is only my concept idea, I have yet to make a complete prototype with all of the aforementioned features.
