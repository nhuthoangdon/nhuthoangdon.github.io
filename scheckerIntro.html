The Brief

Concept: a personalised, inclusive, accessible healthcare system that helps managing symptoms and diseases. Not just a symptom checker, it aims to provide transparency in diagnostic and inform users about their conditions
Deliverables: conceptualisation, experimental design, app mockup

How it works

1. Symptom Assessment

AI-employed symptom checker function, tailored questions for individual circumstances following same procedures as a real physician

Find, book, and manage appointments with relevant physicians/clinics. Assessment questionnaires and results will be sent to appointed doctor(s) upon confirmed bookings

2. Appoinment Booking
3. Symptom & Medication Management

Keep track of diagnosed & undiagnosed symptoms/diseases together with their relevant medication/treatments
