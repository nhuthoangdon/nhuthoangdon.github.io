The idea

AI has been integrated in more and more walks of life, including the healthcare industry. The idea of this app is to tackle the self-diagnosing problem in patients, act as a bridge between patients and their doctors, as well as aiding in the management of a condition. The ambition of this platform is to educate the general public about health issues and connect them with healthcare professionals in a streamlined process.

How it works

Get the Assessment

Schecker's algorithm assesses the symptoms with the same approach as a physician. The AI requests and analyses the user's inputs and info (medical history, gender, age, diets, etc.) to seek the link(s) between those and matching conditions.

Book and manage appointments effortlessly. Results from the assessment and patient's info will be sent to doctors prior to the appointments for a quicker diagnosis process.

Book Appointments with Chosen Physicians
Track and Manage Symptoms / Medication

Keep track of the diseases, symptoms and their medication with custom reminders to never miss a dose.
