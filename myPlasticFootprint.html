An app to track your plastic usage.

CONCEPT

As the environmental issues associating with plastic are increasing, it seems one of the most possible solutions that everyone can do at the moment is to reduce/reuse/recycle their plastic usage, and to do that effectively, I think a tool to track our plastic usage would help.

The concept is rather simple, so is the app's interface. For each and every item that contains plastic bought and/or thrown away, users mark their (negative) impacts; and vice versa, for each and every plastic item recycled, users mark their (positive) impacts. The goal is to either balance out the impacts (neutral), or better yet - to create positive ones.

Illustration designed by macrovector / Freepik
HOW IT WORKS
Mark Impacts

Tap on the red foot if you just bought and/or threw away a new piece of plastic, green foot if you just recycled or reused. Buying a new piece of plastic creates 1 negative impact, throwing it away doubles the impact, so if you buy, for instance, a plastic water bottle and then throw it away, you must mark your negative impacts two times.

Acknowledge the plastic

You can also put in the plastic numbers/abbreviations/names to get an estimation on how much of an impact you just made, as well as recycling info and suggestions.

Set goal & Compete

Set goals and motivate yourself by competing with others.
