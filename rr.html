The Brief

UX/UI enhancements. UX solutions on the online forms for optimal user experience and drive application rate.

Project Type
UX/UI research & enhancements.

My Role
UX/UI design.

THE CHALLENGE

Making a complex process pain-free. Heavy information, multiple integrations.

The Application

Each step of the application process was designed base on insights from user research and client's requirements. The process required quite many inputs and verifications which could be a hassle for customers if not presented well. The solutions were to break the process down to step by step and arrange the required information by common orders.

The Profile

Customer Profile contained complex information, features and details, thus very content-heavy. The page was designed with vertical section navigator to the left (desktop), and accordions for contents to the right. Many other UI components were also used. UI was kept as simple as possible for optimal content loading speed.
