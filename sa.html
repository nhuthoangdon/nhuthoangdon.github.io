Standards Australia

Standards Australia is Australia's leading non-governmental, non-profit standards organisation. They were looking for a new look and feel of their website, in which I had the chance to participate in the visual design process.

Project type
Website re-design.

My Role
Visual design.

THE CHALLENGE

The client was very particular about the display and hiarachy of contents, as well as demanding on getting every page properly prototyped interactively to the details before the web development. I took part in the website's visual design, and was responsible for the prototyping and communicating with developers to ensure the live site would be consistent with its prototype.
