The Brief

Improve current UX/UI and implement new features for the centralised B2B eCommerce platform, customisation for individual countries/OpCos within the APAC region

Project Type
B2B eCommerce, webapp & mobile app

Deliverables
Interactive mockups/prototypes

CHALLENGES

Balancing stakeholder demands and system consistency, capacity, resources, and development efforts. Support & optimisation for older, non-tech-savvy audience (main users)

On-boarding Flow

Task(s):
Optimise the Login and Registration process. Redesign the onboarding flows (Login, Register, Forgot Password) to be up to par with UX best practice. Refine UI to suit intended audience, mobile-first approach

Approach(es):
• UX research (informal), constructed detailed flow diagram
• Simplified steps, limited amount of information
• Phone number and OTP login/register integration
• Flexible input options
• Simple, lean UI, accommodating bigger text display

Ordering Experience

Task(s):
Redefine and amend complex order flows for consumers and distributors, ensuring consistency of orders between the audiences. Tailor/add functionalities for individual OpCos

Approach(es):
• Redesigned user flows
• Identified and resolved discrepancies and incoherence between flows
• Enhanced checkout process & information hierachy
• Revamped UI for clearer/more transparent displays

Order Details & Management

Task(s):
Update and refine order management flows on consumer and distributor interfaces. Tailor functionalities as per region requirements

Approach(es):
• Fine-tuned user flows
• Ensured consistency between ordering flows and order management flows
• Revamped UI

The Rest

Other major & minor flows/twists including account management, incentive management, stock management, and more
