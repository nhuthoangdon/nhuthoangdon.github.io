A social network for those in special circumstances to make a wish.

CONCEPT

Growing up close to my grandparents, I have developed a deep empathy and much respect to the elderly. And after many conversations with different elders (plus that cutest movie "Up"), I realised that while everyone seems to have at least a lifetime wish, not all are lucky enough to fulfil it during their younger years, and some wishes might sound surprisingly simple and easy to some people, yet impossible to others. So I came up with this idea to give those over 70, or anyone under a special circumstance in which they are unable to fulfil their lifetime wishes while their life clock is ticking (e.g. a deadly disease) another chance.

The concept is that, everyone can join the network, but only those that meet the requirements (there will be a verification process) can post a wish (others will also be able to nominate and post in behalf of a qualified person too). Then the community of all users would 'vote', donate or offer help to the one whose story touches them the most, donated funds will be used to help those people accomplishing their lifetime wishes.

HOW IT WORKS
Mark Impacts

Tap on the red foot if you just bought and/or threw away a new piece of plastic, green foot if you just recycled or reused. Buying a new piece of plastic creates 1 negative impact, throwing it away doubles the impact, so if you buy, for instance, a plastic water bottle and then throw it away, you must mark your negative impacts two times.
