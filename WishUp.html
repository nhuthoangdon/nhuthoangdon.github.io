A social network for those in special circumstances to make a wish.

CONCEPT

Growing up close to my grandparents, I have developed a deep empathy and much respect to the elderly. And after many conversations with different elders (plus that cute movie "Up"), I realised that while everyone seems to have at least a lifetime wish, not all are lucky enough to fulfil it during their younger years, and some wishes might sound surprisingly simple and easy to some people, yet impossible to others. So I came up with this idea to give those over 70, or anyone under a special circumstance (e.g. a deadly disease) in which they are unable to fulfil their lifetime wishes while their life clock is ticking another chance.

The concept is that, everyone can join the network, but only those that meet the requirements (there will be a verification process) can post a wish (others will also be able to nominate and post in behalf of a qualified person too). Then the community of all users would 'like', donate or offer help to the one(s) whose stories touches them the most, donated funds will be used to help those people accomplishing their lifetime wishes.

HOW IT WORKS
Wish luck - Donate - Sponsor

The app works similar to other social networks, but users will be swiping through wishes (unfulfilled & fulfilled). Once a 'wisher' gets enough funding and/or 'likes', we will help him/her to use it for his/her wish.

This idea is still in development, a prototype has yet to be made.
