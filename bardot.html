The Brief

New eCommerce website with a refreshing look and user-centered design, new shopping experience to drive conversion rate forward. Mobile-first approach

Project Type
B2C eCommerce, UX/UI design

Deliverables
Interactive prototype

CHALLENGES

Micro interactions, out-of-scope functionalities, mobile optimisation for heavy contents

SEARCH Enhancement

Search is an important function for most platforms, even more so for eCommerce. The first approach in the redesign process was to reconstruct the header, with a focus on the Search function

SEARCH SUGGESTION

Analysed common keywords and search behaviours of targeted end users and designed a search suggestion strategy that offered visual and textual hints that aligned with the marketing agenda, while influencing user decisions

Header Revamp

Heatmaps from user research showed the majority of interactions occured within the top. Our team decluttered the old header, got rid of redundancy, and highlighted mandatory items

DROPDOWN MENU UI

Redesigned category taxonomy and enhanced dropdown menu's UI

Micro Interactions

UX/UI solutions for additional features and functions such as locating a relevant store, lookbook, stock checker

Visual Design

Visual design for product badges and other graphic elements

Creation & amendments on primary & secondary pages

Order & Account management flows
