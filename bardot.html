The Brief

Bardot was looking for a new, refreshed design for their eCommerce website. Our teams' mission was to optimise the current site's UX/UI and deliver a new shopping experience that would drive conversion rate forward.

Platform
Responsive web.

Deliveries
Interactive Prototype.

CHALLENGES

Micro interactions, out-of-scope functionalities, mobile optimisation for heavy contents.

SEARCH Enhancement

Search is an important function for most platforms, even more so for eCommerce. The first approach in the redesign process was to reconstruct the header, with a focus on the Search function.

SEARCH SUGGESTION

Analysed and suggested search algorithms as a result of the collaboration between UX research, design, and development teams.

Header Revamp

Heatmaps from user research showed the majority of interactions happened within the top. We decluttered the old header, removed unnecessary items, and made mandatory ones easily accessible.

DROPDOWN MENU UI

Redesigned the Dropdown Menu, enhanced its UI, sorted the categories with a better taxonomy.

LOCATE IN-STORE Feature

Last-minute out-of-scope function for an in-store availability quick check. A quick Popup served perfectly well for this purpose.

PRODUCT BADGES

Designed visually distinguished badges for applicable product tiles.
